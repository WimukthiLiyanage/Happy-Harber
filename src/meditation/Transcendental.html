Transcendental Meditation

How to Practice Transcendental Meditation:

Transcendental Meditation is a technique that involves silently repeating a mantra to achieve a state of relaxed awareness.

To begin, find a comfortable and quiet place to sit. Close your eyes and begin silently repeating a specific mantra. Let your mind naturally settle into a state of inner peace and tranquility.

Continue this practice for about 15-20 minutes, twice a day, preferably in the morning and evening. Allow your mind to gently return to normal waking consciousness after your meditation session.

This practice aims to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and enhance self-awareness.

  1. Find a quiet and comfortable place

    Choose a calm and peaceful environment where you can sit comfortably.

  2. Repeat a mantra silently

    Choose a simple and specific mantra and silently repeat it during your meditation session.

  3. Practice regularly

    Engage in this practice consistently, twice a day, for optimal benefits.

  4. Allow natural awareness

    Allow your mind to naturally settle into a state of relaxed awareness during the meditation.

Benefits of Transcendental Meditation:

  • Promotes relaxation and reduces stress

    Transcendental Meditation helps in achieving a state of deep relaxation, reducing stress levels and promoting overall well-being.

  • Enhances self-awareness

    Regular practice of this meditation technique increases self-awareness and mindfulness.

  • Improves overall mental clarity

    Transcendental Meditation contributes to enhanced mental clarity and focus in daily life.
